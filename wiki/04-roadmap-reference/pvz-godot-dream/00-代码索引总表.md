## 快速定位指南

### "我想看某个植物/僵尸的实现"
→ `scripts/character/plant/plant_XXX_name.gd` 或 `scripts/character/zombie/zombie_XXX_name.gd`

### "我想看角色组件（攻击/血量/动画）"
→ `scripts/character/components/` 下的子目录，详见 [03-组件系统](03-组件系统.md)

### "我想看子弹/投射物"
→ `scripts/bullet/` 和 `scenes/bullet/`

### "我想看事件系统"
→ `scripts/autoload/event_bus.gd`

### "我想看场景如何注册和实例化"
→ `scripts/autoload/scene_registry.gd` 和 `scripts/autoload/global/character_registry.gd`

### "我想看主战斗流程"
→ `scripts/manager/main_game_manager.gd` 及其子管理器目录

### "我想看卡牌/手牌系统"
→ `scripts/manager/card_manager.gd`、`scripts/manager/hand_manager/`、`scenes/ui/all_cards/`

### "我想看关卡配置"
→ `data/ChooesLevel.tres`、`level_game_para/`、`scripts/resources/level/`

### "我想看着色器"
→ `shaders/` 目录，粒子 shader 使用 `.gdshaderinc` 包含文件
